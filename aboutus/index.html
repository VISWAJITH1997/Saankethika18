About Us

R.S MUNIRATHINAM

Founder-Chairman


Thiru.R.S.Munirathinam Founder-Chairman of R.M.K Group of Institutions is well known for his philanthropic attitude and service as an elected member of the State Assembly of Tamil Nadu. He envisioned a technological revolution and committed himself to establish an institution, to foster technical education. He is a rare breed of educationalist, for whom quality is the way of life. A compassionate and modest person to associate with, he is quite undeterred in his conviction in executing anything to perfection that is for common good. The lush green environment, the expansive buildings, the well-equipped laboratories and the unparalleled discipline of the students standout as shining examples of his unique style of management. Thanks to the full time participation of the Management and the guidance provided by learned professors, the college has achieved great heights. The Management believes that the nation needs not just engineers and professionals, but virtuous men and women who can be engineers and professionals. Integrity of character and discipline are imparted to students to become good citizens capable of utilizing their talents for the development of our Nation. Our main concern is to ensure the development of students in the best educational environment which reinforces the traditional values of our society. This college is dedicated and committed to give the best to the community as another premier institution shaping tomorrow's engineers, technologists, technocrats and managers.

Thiru.R.M. Kishore- Vice Chairman


Shri. R.M.Kishore is a Mechanical Engineer from the University of Madras and has acquired his Masters degree in Business administration from University of Huddersfield, UK. He is a young and energetic member of the management team and is active in managing the RMK group of Educational Institutions.

Dr. N.Anbuchezhian- The Principal


Dr. N.Anbuchezhian possesses a rich experience of 31 years in Teaching, Research and Administration in the affiliated colleges of Anna University, Chennai. He has served as the Vice-Principal, Academic Coordinator, Head of the Department (Mechanical Engineering) and Convener of Examination Cell in Kongu Engineering College & R.M.K. Engineering College. He has been a visiting faculty of Anna University, Chennai. He was appointed as the Chairman of valuation board - Zone I in Anna University, Chennai and was also a member of the Examinations Reforms Committee of Anna University of Technology, Coimbatore. He has successfully completed two MODROB projects worth Rs.19 lakhs from the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi. He has contributed over 10 publications in refereed Journals and Conference proceedings. He has led more than 30 UG and PG research projects. He has convened three International and four National Conferences. He is a life time member of the Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE), Member of the Institution of Engineers (India), and Member of the All India Management Association (AIMA).
